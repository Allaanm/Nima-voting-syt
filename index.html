Nima voting is a secure online voting platform that makes it easy to vote for their leaders from anywhere at the comfort of their places.We help achieve more democratic outcomes by better representing the will of our voters.


What Entails our Voting

Online Election

Voters can vote at the comfort of their home.

Automated Ballot Counts

Votes are counted automatically and displayed.

Easy to Set Up and Vote


We make it super easy for our students to set an election and to cast their votes.


When the election starts, each of the registered voter is allowed to secretly login. The voter then just clicks that link to be immediately taken to the voting page. Because our process is so easy for voters, we get a higher percentage of voters to actually vote.


Our interface allows both regular (check box) voting. You can try the voting interface in the example ballots that follow or by casting a vote in our demonstration poll.

Transparent Results


Students love the clear and transparent results. Nima voting will enable you to provide them with such transparency. At the end of your election, a detailed report with counts automatically done for you.


Security Information

No attack

We offer the strongest possible guarantee of voter anonymity: we simply do not track who votes for what; only who votes, and what the vote is

Encryption

Our voting sites are encrypted so our voters can enjoy complete security and privacy while voting.

Private
